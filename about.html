Roles

  • Director ☆☆☆ The Owner, CEO, Founder of the server.
  • Deputy Director ☆☆ The Co-Owner, COO, Co-Founder of the server.
  • Senior Public Secretary ☆ The Manager, Highest Admin and Highest Staff for the server.
  • Senior Secretary Higher Admin for the server.
  • Secretary Admin for the server.
  • Employee Moderator for the server.
  • Trial Employee Trial Staff/Moderator for the server.
  • Employer A trusted staff member, trusted by a Senior Staff (☆) of the server, access to all channels.
  • Citizens A general member of the server access to the full public community, not all channels.

Promotion Guidelines

  • Senior Public Secretary — 15,000 Points
  • Senior Secretary — 10,000 Points
  • Secretary — 8,000 Points
  • Employee — 5,000 Points
  • Trial Staff — You must fill in an application and recieve 3,000 points in accordance with the requirements.
  • Employer — You must be trusted by a Senior Staff member to recieve this role.

By doing one or more of these, you may recieve points, once you recieve the amount of points required for your role, please contact a Sr. Staff. More information on the requirements for these roles will be here. This is just a basic summary.
