Community Guidelines

Read our server's rules and regulations below!

Follow Discord Terms and Guidelines

Abide by the Discord's terms and guidelines at all times. This includes, but is not limited to, refraining from sharing illegal content, engaging in unauthorized activities, or violating Discord's policies. It is within Discord's Terms & Guidelines that you must be atleast 13 years of age to be on Discord. If we catch you not abiding by the Terms and Guidelines of Discord, we will be forced to ban you from our server.

Be Respectful and Inclusive

Treat others with respect at all times. Discrimination, hate speech, harassment, or any forms of bullying will not be tolerated. If you're being disrespectful, we allow minimum one reclamation, yet even so, you will be muted for 24h, and if it continues we will be forced to ban you immediately.

Keep it SFW

Keep all content, discussions, and interactions appropriate for users aged 13 and above. This includes refraining from explicit, sexually suggestive, violence or otherwise NSFW content. As stated above, same for this rule, we allow one reclamation, you will be given 2 warns as a whole, and number increasing depending on the rate of NSFW content you have sent, if it is escalated we will be forced to mute you, even ban you.

Do not share or request personal information from others without their explicit consent. We have an intro channel and if they are comfortable with sharing extra information, they may so. DO NOT force anyone to give you their personal information, such as address and more. Respect others' privacy and refrain from doxing, stalking, or engaging in any form of invasive behavior. This can be punished first-hand with a mute, even if you can reclaim, we will be forced to ban you depending on the adversity of the situation.
